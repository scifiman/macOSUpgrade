Example of Required Self Service Description

This process will upgrade your Mac to MacOS Catalina 10.15.4.

*Before proceeding...*
* Please ensure __all work is saved__
* Close any apps you may have open

_This process will take 30-90 minutes to complete, during which time you will not be able to use your Mac._

Example of Factory Reset Self Service Description

This process will erase and re-install MacOS Catalina 10.15.4 on your Mac.

*Before proceeding...*
* Please ensure __all work is saved__
* Close any apps you may have open

_This process will take 30-90 minutes to complete, during which time you will not be able to use your Mac._
